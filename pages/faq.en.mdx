### What is Rubicon?

Rubicon is an open platform and protocol for trading [ERC-20](https://ethereum.org/en/developers/docs/standards/tokens/erc-20/) tokens, built on  Ethereum.

To start trading, visit [**app.rubicon.finance**](https://app.rubicon.finance/).

### What chains/networks are supported?

Rubicon is live on [**Optimism**](https://www.optimism.io/), [**Base**](https://www.base.org/), [**Arbitrum**](https://arbitrum.io/), [**Ethereum Mainnet**](https://ethereum.org/) and will be on more networks in the near future!

### What tokens are supported?

The Rubicon protocol supports trading of any token that adheres to the [ERC-20 standard](https://ethereum.org/en/developers/docs/standards/tokens/erc-20/).

### Where can I get support?

For support, join the Rubicon [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/E7pS24J) server and create a support ticket in the **#create-ticket** channel. Be careful of scammers and impersonators, our team will never message you first or ask for your seed phrase or private keys!

### Does Rubicon have a token?

Rubicon does not currently have a token, we plan to decentralize the protocol as soon as possible. Any announcements will come from the Rubicon team on official channels.
